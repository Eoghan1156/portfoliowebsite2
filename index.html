A B O U T

Welcome to my portfolio site!My name is Eoghan. I'm a 21 year old student based in Armagh, Northern Ireland, currently studying at the University of Ulster Art college and I have a passion for all things illustrative!

Drawing is my obsession! Ever since I could hold a pencil in my hand I've been drawing. Characterture is my favourite aspect of illustration, Inventing worlds from a pencil and a piece of paper. My life goal is to be a reknown illustrator.
Music has always been a lifelong passion of mine. I'm obsessed with learning different instruments, even when I end up not being very good. When I'm not doodling I'm making music on my guitar or waking up the neighbours with my harmonica.
Gaming isn't just a hobby for me. I'm fascinated with how video game companies invent worlds, the thought processes behind it , and the endless amounts of gaming experiences that can be achieved with the power of the mind.
